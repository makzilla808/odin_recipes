Creamy Clam Dip

Super easy clam dip recipe is the perfect party snack!

Ingredients

  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans chopped clams
  • 1 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp yellow mustard powder

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth, adding juice from the clams as you beat.
  2. Stir in clams.
  3. Stir in white pepper and mustard powder.
  4. Chill for 1-2 hours.
  5. Serve with crackers or chips.
