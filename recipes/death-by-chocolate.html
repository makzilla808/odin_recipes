Death By Chocolate

Chocolatey Dessert

Deliciously amazing layered chocolate dessert.

Ingredients

  • 1 package chocolate cake mix
  • 1 package instant chocolate pudding
  • 2 package frozen cool whip, thawed
  • 3 Skor bars or any chocolate covered toffee bar, crumbled
  • 1 package Oreos, crumbled

Steps

  1. Prepare chocolate cake mix according to package directions. Let cool.
  2. Prepare pudding according to directions on package.
  3. In a large glass bowl, layer in the following order: 1/3 of the cake, crumbled; 1/3 of the pudding; 1/3 of the crushed oreos; 1 toffee bar,crushed; 1/3 of the cool whip.
  4. Repeat layers in the same order creating 3 layers. Crumble the last toffee bar on top.
  5. Refrigerate for a few hours or overnight.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
